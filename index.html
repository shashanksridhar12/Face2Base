A group of teenagers fighting a cause

A few months ago, a close friend's grandma was diagnosed with dementia. While its hard to believe it is common, over 47 million people in the world suffer from this disorder. In fact, every year, ten million people are added to this number. While our friend's grandma is happy as she could be, so much is lost through her inability to communicate the same way with others.

The number one symptom of dementia and alzheimer's is memory loss. It directly leads to a unstable realtionship with those they love. People who suffer this symptom simply cannot sustain the same relationships as before.

About Us

Reidius is a social computer application that aids individuals who feel unsafe, or uncomfortable while being outside. The application includes multiple features that help in situations of harassment or bullying.


When someone feels unsafe outside, they can press a button and notify the police.
